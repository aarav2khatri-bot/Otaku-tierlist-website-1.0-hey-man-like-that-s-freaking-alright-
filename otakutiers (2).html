About OtakuTiers

What is OtakuTiers?

The definitive Minecraft PvP ranking platform — tracking the best players across NethPot, Sword, UHC, DiaPot, Vanilla, SMP, Mace and Axe PvP.

Powered by a live Supabase global database. Rankings update in real time worldwide.

Tier System

HT = High Tier (HT1 best → HT5). LT = Low Tier (LT1 → LT5). Points: HT1=20, HT2=17, HT3=14, HT4=11, HT5=8, LT1=6, LT2=4, LT3=3, LT4=2, LT5=1.

🟢 Green bar = promoted since last update. 🔴 Red bar = demoted since last update.

FAQ

How do I get ranked? ▼
Use the Submit page. Staff review evidence and add you via the Admin panel.
What do the green/red bars mean? ▼
Green = player was promoted to this tier recently. Red = player was demoted. Set by admin when editing.
Can I dispute a placement? ▼
Yes — join our Discord and open a dispute ticket with evidence.
